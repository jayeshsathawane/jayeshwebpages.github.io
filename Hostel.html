About Hostel

The Hostel Nagzira started in the year 2001.Having Intake capacity of 176 students. Hostel has many facilities for students such as Solar Panel is available in the hostel so students can get hot water whenever required. Water purifier for providing healthy drinking water.Monthly mess on cooperative Basis. Medical facility is also provided on regular basis.Different activities are organized in the hostel like Lord Ganesh Festival, Teachers Day,Engineers Day etc.
